Chapter I

The Obsidian Origin

Before the kiln, before the wheel, there was the volcano. Our journey begins in the desolate beauty of the highlands, where the earth breathes fire and stone is born from chaos.

The Obsidian Series is an homage to this raw power. We sought to capture not just the color, but the feeling of volcanic glass—cool to the touch, heavy with history, and possessing a depth that absorbs light rather than reflecting it.

02 Chapter II

The Maker's Mark

In an era of mass production, we choose the path of resistance. Every plate, every bowl carries the faint, deliberate imprint of the human hand.

I.

The Conversation of Clay

The clay is not forced into shape; it is guided. Our artisans spend years learning to listen to the material, finding the balance between structure and fluidity.

II.

Shadow & Glaze

Our signature 'Obsidian' glaze is applied in layers. It is designed to interact with candlelight, creating a dining landscape of deep shadows and subtle highlights.

III.

Trial by Fire

At 1,300°C, the object is reborn. The heat transforms the fragile earth into stone-like porcelain, sealing the maker's intent forever.

fingerprint

Imperfection as Signature

We do not reject slight variations; we celebrate them. A subtle asymmetry in the rim, a unique pooling of glaze—these are the fingerprints of the process, ensuring your collection is truly one of a kind.
